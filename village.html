About Madaj Gram Panchayat

Welcome to Madaj, a beautiful village in Osmanabad district of Maharashtra. Our village is known for its rich culture, hard-working people, and peaceful environment. Agriculture is the main work of most families here, and farming supports the life of the village. The Gram Panchayat of Madaj works for the progress and development of the village. It takes care of roads, water supply, cleanliness, schools, health services, and government schemes. The Panchayat also supports farmers, helps in festivals, and looks after social welfare activities. Madaj has schools, temples, health facilities, and other services that make life easier for the people. The village also celebrates important festivals like Diwali, Dasara, Gauri Pujan, and Bail Pola with unity and happiness.

The total geographical area of the village is 2178.79R. Madaj has a total population of 5409, with 2841 males and 2568 females. Literacy rate is 77.35% (82.33% males, 72.20% females). There are about 1255 houses, and the Pincode is 413606.
