About Our Village

Welcome to Madaj, a beautiful village in Osmanabad district of Maharashtra. Our village is known for its rich culture, hard-working people, and peaceful environment. Agriculture is the main work of most families here, and farming supports the life of the village. The Gram Panchayat of Madas works for the progress and development of the village. It takes care of roads, water supply, cleanliness, schools, health services, and government schemes....
